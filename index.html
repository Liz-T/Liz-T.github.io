Fragmented Network

I have a desire to capture things, one way or another.

It's always interesting. It's mostly fun. At times it's exhausting.

I log, in a variety of ways, images, thoughts and ideas. Both those I make and those I find.

Fragmented network is an attempt to gather together, organise and hopefully provide some relief for my brain.

Here you'll find links to things such as:

  • projects; finished, in progress or not started
  • 'notes'; text, images or sound captured in sketchbooks, on recording devises or with cameras
  • ideas and thoughts on a variety of things I have created, or read or listen to
